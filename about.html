Our Origins

Leadership

ANANT AGRAWAL

Founder & Lead Architect

Anant Agrawal Architects was founded with a singular vision: to redefine architecture by harmonizing cutting-edge innovation with the principles of sustainability and tradition. Our story is one of rebirth—a commitment to building spaces that honor the environment while pushing the boundaries of what's possible in modern design.

Inspired by the blend of cultural heritage and forward-thinking technology, we seek to challenge conventional practices. We create living spaces that breathe, adapt, and regenerate—offering both functionality and a lasting positive impact on urban landscapes.


Academically, I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Architecture (B.Arch) from Mumbai University, India, and a Master’s in Sustainable Mega Buildings (MSc) from the prestigious Cardiff University, UK—ranked among the top 5 universities globally in Architecture by QS World Rankings. This academic foundation, combined with hands-on experience, allows me to approach each project with a balance of creativity, technical precision, and global perspective.

This journey is ongoing, driven by the belief that good architecture shapes not just skylines but the way we live, work, and connect. At Anant Agrawal Architects, we partner closely with clients, guiding them through every phase—from concept development to execution—to realize exceptional architectural experiences grounded in innovation and integrity.

The Process

During my Sustainable Mega Buildings studies at Cardiff University’s Welsh School of Architecture, I participated in an advanced urban climate walk through London’s high-rise district, guided by Dr. Julie (Ann) Futcher. This experience, alongside fellow students and Professors Tania Sharmin and Eshrar Latif, deepened my expertise in analyzing how tall buildings influence complex urban microclimates.


It enhanced my ability to design high-rise projects that thoughtfully respond to their environmental context, optimizing both performance and occupant comfort. This hands-on exposure underscores my commitment to delivering sophisticated, climate-aware architecture informed by cutting-edge research and real-world observation.
